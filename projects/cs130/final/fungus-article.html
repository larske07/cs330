Fungus Fanaticism

Can you imagine how amazing it would have been had I actually made an entire article all about fungi? I mean, that would have been pretty cool. Of course, I suppose it would also be funny if I had just made my own sort of Lorem Ipsum type dummy text to replace the article about fungi. It would also be kinda hilarious if I made it meta and within the text I gave a play-by-play of what I am talking about right now. But then again, I would have to rewrite it over again for the three articles and that might get tedious. Wait, what am I talking about I could just copy and paste and change the text so that evertime I say something like fungi I can just change it from fungi to something else. Actually, now that I think about it that would save a lot of time so long as I dont say fungi too many times. I wonder if someone will actually bother to read all of the text that I write here though. After all, that would be truly tedious compared to my whole don't say fungi too many times thing. Dang, how many times have I said fungi? I should really stop before it becomes too much work. I guess this is as good a spot as any to stop writing. I may as well say fungi one more time: fungi.

We're bananas about these bugs!
